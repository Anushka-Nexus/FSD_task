Corjuem Fort

Corjuem Fort is an example of the fact that forts need not be huge to be effective and travel-friendly

Reis Magos Fort

Among the ancient forts in Goa is Reis Magos. The fort, located on the banks of Mandovi river, was built in 1551.

Sinquerim Fort

The small fort is an extension of Aguada Fort, and is located in close proximity to Panaji.

Aguada Fort

Aguada Fort is probably among the biggest tourist attractions in Goa.
